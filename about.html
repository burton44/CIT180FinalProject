My name is Richard Burton and I live in Leelanau County, MI. I am studying Computer Technology Development at Northwestern Michigan College. I have a degree from MSU in Environmental Science/Zoology and I now work at Tandem Ciders in Suttons Bay. I direct our three man production crew in creating delicious hard ciders. We receive the fruit from growers and from there we press, ferment, monitor, filter and package hard cider. It is a lot of hard work, but creating a delicous beverage that people enjoy is very rewarding. I hope to use my skills learned at NMC to help Tandem integrate new technologies into all aspects of the business. I also hope to find a second job in the exciting and expanding field of computer programming!
